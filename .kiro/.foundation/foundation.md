## **3\. Solution: YieldQuest**

YieldQuest introduces a **Quest-based Yield Game**, where:

* Users **deposit into real yield vaults**

* Yield is converted into:

  * XP

  * Levels

  * Badges

  * Social reputation

* Players compete individually or in guilds

* Progress is transparent and verifiable on-chain

### **4.1 Real Yield First**

* All rewards are backed by **actual yield**

* Yield comes from Mantle-native strategies

### **4.2 Game Progression, Not Token Inflation**

* Rewards emphasize:

  * XP

  * Levels

  * Status

### **4.3 Social & Competitive**

## **5\. User Journey (High-Level)**

1. User connects wallet

2. Chooses a Quest (Yield Strategy)

3. Deposits assets into a vault

4. Earns yield over time

5. Yield converts to XP & progression

6. Unlocks badges, ranks, and social status

7. Appears on leaderboards / guild rankings

---

## **6\. Game Mechanics**

### **6.1 Quests**

* Each Quest \= one yield strategy

* Examples:

  * Stable Yield Quest

  * LP Quest

  * Long-term Lock Quest

### **6.2 XP & Leveling**

* XP earned based on:

  * Yield amount

  * Time commitment

  * Quest difficulty

* Levels unlock:

  * Visual upgrades

  * Access to advanced quests

  * Higher leaderboard weight

### **6.3 Badges & Achievements**

* On-chain badges (non-transferable)

* Examples:

  * Early Adopter

  * Yield Master

  * Top 10% Guild Contributor

## **8\. Smart Contract Architecture (High-Level)**

### **8.1 Core Contracts**

* **QuestVault (ERC-4626)**

  * Handles deposits & yield generation

* **XPManager**

  * Converts yield into XP

  * Applies multipliers & caps

* **BadgeNFT**

  * Non-transferable achievement NFTs

* **LeaderboardSnapshot**

  * Records periodic rankings

  * Optimized for read access

### **8.2 Design Principles**

* Modular

* Composable

* Minimal trust assumptions

* Event-driven for off-chain indexing
